About Mangla

We are in the Lifting Businesses™, serving a broad range of customers in industrial sectors like Steel, Power, Cement, Paper, Chemicals & Fertilizers, Automotive, Construction and other Engineering fields since the past 50 years.

Mangla Hoists Pvt Ltd is one of the oldest Business Partners of ‘INDEF’ since 1973. We have been Business Partners with ‘USHA MARTIN’ since 2015.

Manufacturers of EOT Cranes, HOT Cranes, Gantry Cranes, and Jib Cranes and installed the Noida factory in 1999. We expanded into the production of Electric Hoists in 2014. Further diversified into the manufacturing of ElectroMagnets in 2017.

We are recognized and approved by leading organizations like BHEL, NTPC, SAIL, Mecon, L&T, GE, ISGEC, RITES, Jal Board, Macawber Beekay & EIL. We’ve been catering to the needs of our customers and consultants in India and abroad, exporting to Africa, Europe, and Middle-East countries.

Customization is our forte. Quality Management is our priority.

Energy-efficient and Intelligent control systems. Cost-efficient, we provide lifelong spares support. We are prompt and provide efficient after-sales service.
